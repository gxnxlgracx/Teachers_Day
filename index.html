"The dream begins, most of the time, with a teacher who believes in you, who tugs and pushes and leads you on the next plateau, sometimes poking you with a sharp stick called truth."

Dan Rather







When we are young, we are unable to distinguish between morality and immorality, good and evil, or right from wrong. We are innocent and oblivious to the ways of the world; in our vulnerability, a teacher holds our hand in the world's busy traffic. Their knowledge and experience serve as a sidewalk on which they lay out explanations of how the world around us functions.


Given the difficulties teachers have endured during the COVID-19 issue, World Teachers' Day this year has an even greater significance. As the pandemic has demonstrated, they play a critical role in guaranteeing academic continuity and promoting students' mental health and wellness.


Even though we do not appreciate our teachers enough, this day provides us with a golden opportunity to shower them with respect, gratitude, and reverence. We'd want to thank our teachers for constantly guiding and pointing us in the proper direction. You are an inspiration to all of us, and we are fortunate to have you as our guide.


We applaud our teachers for being a beacon of light in the dark ambiguities of life's ever-uncertain journey. Thank you, and Happy Teachers Day once more!
